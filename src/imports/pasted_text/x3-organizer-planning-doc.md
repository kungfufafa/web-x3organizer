IMPORTANT CONTEXT

Website ini adalah redesign untuk X3 Organizer dengan tujuan:

- Memperjelas layanan perjalanan dan organizer
- Meningkatkan kepercayaan calon pelanggan
- Melayani segmen korporat, kampus/institusi, keluarga, dan peserta trip
- Memudahkan pengunjung menemukan layanan atau paket yang sesuai
- Mengarahkan pengunjung menuju konsultasi WhatsApp
- Memiliki tampilan clean, terang, modern, dan profesional
- Tetap menggunakan unsur navy, blue, gold, dan silver dari logo

PRIMARY CONVERSION

Konsultasi perjalanan melalui WhatsApp.

SECONDARY CONVERSIONS

- Melihat layanan
- Menjelajahi paket atau destinasi
- Mengisi brief kebutuhan perjalanan
- Melihat dokumentasi dan pengalaman perjalanan
- Membaca artikel perjalanan
- Mengikuti akun sosial resmi

PLANNING OBJECTIVES

Create a clear and conversion-oriented website architecture that
supports both visitors who already know what they need and visitors
who still need help choosing a trip.

Plan the website around user needs, not around internal company
departments.

REQUIRED USER JOURNEYS

Create user journeys for:

1. Corporate representative looking for a company gathering
2. Campus or institution planning a group trip
3. Family planning a customized trip
4. Individual looking for an open trip
5. Visitor entering through a travel article
6. Visitor who is unsure which service is appropriate

For every journey explain:

- Entry point
- Main question
- Information required
- Trust element required
- Recommended CTA
- Final conversion path

REQUIRED SITEMAP

Determine the recommended sitemap and explain whether these pages
should exist, be combined, or be excluded:

- Home
- Layanan
- Service detail pages
- Paket
- Package detail pages
- Destinasi
- Destination detail pages
- Experience or Gallery
- Tentang X3
- Blog or Travel Insights
- Article detail pages
- Contact
- Travel brief form
- FAQ

Do not create unnecessary pages simply to make the sitemap look large.

HOMEPAGE ARCHITECTURE

Create the recommended homepage section order.

For every section provide:

- Section name
- Purpose
- Main message
- Required content
- Suggested CTA
- Trust element
- Destination page after interaction
- Mobile behavior
- Content dependency

The homepage should consider:

1. Header and navigation
2. Hero
3. Audience or service entry points
4. Core services
5. Featured packages or destinations
6. Reasons to choose X3
7. How the service works
8. Travel documentation or experience
9. Verified testimonials placeholder
10. Travel insights
11. FAQ
12. Final CTA
13. Footer

Do not include a section if it has no clear role in the customer
journey.

SERVICE ARCHITECTURE

- Audience entry points

Services to evaluate:

- Family Gathering

PACKAGE AND DESTINATION ARCHITECTURE

Define the difference between:

- Service
- Package
- Destination
- Travel article
- Travel experience or portfolio

Prevent duplicate content between these content types.

WHATSAPP CONVERSION STRATEGY

Plan:

- Global WhatsApp CTA
- Contextual WhatsApp CTA
- Mobile sticky CTA
- Prefilled message structure
- CTA labels for different pages
- Alternative conversion for visitors not ready to chat

Do not use the same CTA label everywhere if context-specific wording
would be clearer.

- Mobile CTA behavior

QUALITY LOOP

Before returning the final plan:

1. Review business alignment.
2. Review audience relevance.
3. Review conversion flow.
4. Review navigation clarity.
5. Remove redundant pages.
6. Remove duplicate sections.
7. Check that every page has one primary purpose.
8. Check that WhatsApp is not overused or visually intrusive.
9. Revise the plan once.
10. Clearly separate decisions from assumptions.
